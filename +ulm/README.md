## ULM Framework for USTB ##

The ULM Framework for USTB is an addon work created by Simon A. Bjørn during his master's thesis. It has been implemented into the USTB with the approval of authors of 3rd-party code, with the clause of additional citation


Use of the ULM Framework is therefor subject to additional citation of the following article:

- Heiles, B., Chavignon, A., Hingot, V., Lopez, P., Teston, E., & Couture, O. (2022). Performance benchmarking of microbubble-localization algorithms for ultrasound localization microscopy. Nature Biomedical Engineering, 6(5), 605–616. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41551-021-00824-8
